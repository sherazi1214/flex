Allah

square stone building in the center of the Great Mosque at Mecca, the site most holy to Muslims and toward which they must face when praying.

Hazrat muhammed saw

Medina,[a] officially Al-Madinah al-Munawwarah (Arabic: المدينة المنورة, romanized: al-Madīnah al-Munawwarah, lit. 'The Luminous City', Hejazi Arabic pronunciation: [al.maˈdiːna al.mʊˈnawːara])

Mola Ali as

Ali Ibn Abi Talib, also known as Mola Ali, was a prominent figure in Islamic history. He was the cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet Muhammad and played a significant role in shaping the early days of Islam..

BiBi zahra

The Prophet of Islam had only one daughter named Fatima. Her mother Khadija had two other daughters from her two earlier marriages. When The Prophet married her, both daughters came with her mother to live in the house of the Prophet.
