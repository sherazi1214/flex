Huzrat muhammed saw


Medina,[a] officially Al-Madinah al-Munawwarah (Arabic: المدينة المنورة, romanized: al-Madīnah al-Munawwarah, lit. 'The Luminous City', Hejazi Arabic pronunciation: [al.maˈdiːna al.mʊˈnawːara]) and also commonly simplified as Madīnah or Madinah (المدينة, al-Madina), is the capital of Medina Province in the Hejaz region of western Saudi Arabia.






Hazrat Ali as


Ali Ibn Abi Talib, also known as Mola Ali, was a prominent figure in Islamic history. He was the cousin and son-in-law of the Prophet Muhammad and played a significant role in shaping the early days of Islam. Throughout his life, Ali was known for his wisdom, bravery, and eloquence in both speech and action.






Hazrat fatima zahra


The Prophet of Islam had only one daughter named Fatima. Her mother Khadija had two other daughters from her two earlier marriages. When The Prophet married her, both daughters came with her mother to live in the house of the Prophet. Hazrat Fatima (sa) was born five years before Bethat when Muhammad (S) was about 35 years old and her mother Khadija was about 50 years old. She has many other titles.
